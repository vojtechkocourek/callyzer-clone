HOW TO INSTALL THE CALLYZER APK ON YOUR ANDROID PHONE
======================================================

You'll have a file called `callyzer-clone.apk` in the `apk\` folder
next to this file. Get it onto your phone and tap to install.

PICK ONE OF THESE METHODS
-------------------------

Method A - USB cable (fastest if you have one)
  1. Plug your phone into the PC.
  2. On the phone, swipe down the notifications, tap the USB
     notification, and pick "File transfer" (MTP) mode.
  3. In Windows File Explorer, your phone shows up like a drive.
     Open it -> Internal Storage -> Download.
  4. Drag `callyzer-clone.apk` into that folder.
  5. On the phone, open the Files app, find `callyzer-clone.apk`
     in Downloads, and tap it.

Method B - Email or messaging app
  1. Email `callyzer-clone.apk` to yourself, or send it through
     WhatsApp / Telegram / Signal to your own number.
  2. Open the email/chat on your phone and tap the attachment.
  3. Tap "Download" if it asks, then tap the file.

Method C - Cloud (Google Drive / OneDrive)
  1. Upload `callyzer-clone.apk` to Drive or OneDrive on your PC.
  2. On the phone, open the same Drive/OneDrive app.
  3. Tap the .apk and choose "Open" or "Install".

WHEN YOU TAP THE APK ON THE PHONE
---------------------------------

The first time you install an APK from a non-Play-Store source,
Android will ask permission. The exact wording varies by phone:

  - "Install unknown apps" -> tap Settings -> turn on
    "Allow from this source" for the app you tapped from
    (Files, Chrome, Drive, etc.) -> Back -> Install.
  - On older phones: Settings -> Security -> "Unknown sources".

After that, tap Install. When it's done, tap Open.

USING THE APP
-------------

1. The first screen asks for the API URL of your dashboard.
   - If you're testing on Wi-Fi at home, set it to your laptop's
     LAN IP (e.g. http://192.168.1.42:3000). To find your IP,
     open PowerShell on the laptop and run: ipconfig
     Look for "IPv4 Address" under your Wi-Fi adapter.
   - The dashboard (the web app from the launcher) must be
     running for the phone to reach it.
2. Sign in with the same demo account, e.g.
     daniel@callyzer.demo / demo123
3. Tap "Grant permission" so the app can read your call log.
4. Tap "Sync now" - new calls appear in the dashboard within
   seconds.

UNINSTALLING
------------

Long-press the Callyzer Clone icon on your home screen ->
App info -> Uninstall. (Or Settings -> Apps -> Callyzer Clone
-> Uninstall.)
